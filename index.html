About Me

I'm Shawn, a 4th year undergrad @ UCLA studying Biology, B.S. and Cognitive Science, B.S., with a Specialization in Computing.

I'm currently lead student web-developer & designer for @ASUCLA and the Population Health Management Intern for UCLA Health FPG.

Previously lead full-stack developer LanguageBird, LLC.

I'm passionate about research, especially in cognitive psychology, where I am currently studying the spatial-relocation memory errors of young adults in regard to risks & rewards, for UCLA's departmental honors thesis in Dr. Alan Castel's Memory and Lifespan Cognition Lab, with Alexander Siegel, M.A..

When not conducting ground-breaking research, my main interests include software development, full-stack web & digital/graphic design, and user-interface & user-experience development — all of which I sport 10+ years of experience in.

At UCLA, some current projects I've worked on include myEMRA®, a software I developed for UCLA Health, and QR Chem, a smart was to share and access 3D molecular structures. (Read Press Release and checkout the Publication in Nature Reviews Chemistry.)

Check out my CV, Website Portfolio, and some of my Github Repos below to learn more about the academic and creative work that I've been up to these past few years.
